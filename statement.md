# Statement of the Project

 Payment Receipt System 

This project, "Payment Receipt System," has been developed under the curriculum of B. Tech in CSE to understand how the digital transaction and generation of receipt take place in real-world applications.

This project involves the design of a simple, efficient, user-friendly system that can automatically generate payment receipts once a sale has taken place.

---

Objective
The main objectives of this project are:
- To provide a digital means of creating receipts for payment.
- To systematize transaction details for storage and retrieval.

- Reduce manual errors while preparing receipts.

- This allows users to keep a safe and accurate record of their payments.

- Understand the working of the backend in handling receipts.
---
Scope of the project
This project covers:
- Collection of customer/user information.

- Processing payment information.

- Auto-generation of receipts in a structured format.
Saving receipts for future reference.

Receipt display and export (PDF/Print).
- Basic data validation for secure transactions.

Problem Definition

Most small businesses and institutions still prepare their receipts manually.

This leads to:

Delays in billing
- Calculation errors
Difficulty in maintaining records
- Inability to provide electronic evidence of payment

A digital system resolves these problems by guaranteeing automation, accuracy, speed, and quick processing.

---
Proposed Solution 
The Payment Receipt System provides:
Creation of a simple interface for inputting payment details.

- Automatic calculation and formatting of receipts.

- Instant generation of receipt documents.

Storage of all receipts in a database.
Easy access to transaction history.
